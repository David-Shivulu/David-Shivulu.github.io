Health & Safety services

Accident and injury investigations

As a highly experienced safety consulting firm, we know that responsibility is key when an incident occurs. Our on-call safety professionals provide on-site investigations to conduct root Cause analysis and hazard elimination.

Combustible dust hazard analysis (DHA)

A DHA is designed to ensure the safety of employees and facilities. Our team of experienced industrial hygienists and engineers can conduct the DHA, explain the best solutions to manage combustible dusts, and help you establish a Dust Management Plan.

Emergency response plans

We have created Emergency Response Plans for power plants, manufacturing facilities, distribution centers, and utilities.

Our Emergency Response Plan Services include:-

  • Comprehensive safety plans to address all significant emergencies with the potential of impacting worker's safety.
  • Regulatory required Risk Management Plans to protect worker's safety, public health, and the environment.

On-site health and safety management

Our on-site services can be set up to meet your specific requirements for a period agreed upon. Our professionals can perform routine functions on site as Health and Safety Managers that include -but not limited to- maintaining regulatory compliance and providing an overall safe environment for others to work in.
These functions may include:

  • Training
  • Meetings
  • Program Development
  • Regulatory Agency Interaction
  • OSHA Log Completion and Injury reporting
  • Accident/Injury Investigation and Reduction
