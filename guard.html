ENROLLMENT AND VERIFICATION OF SECURITY SUPERVISORS AND SECURITY GUARDS

SELECTION CRITERIA

The Selection Criteria provides necessary framework and minimum requirements for the selection of security supervisors/guards. Security supervisors/guards must comply with the requirements of their clients as well, which may exceed the minimum requirements as set forth in this guideline:-

SUBJECT CRITERIA
General requirements Candidates must be of between 25 and 45 years of age with provisions that the candidate must be able to perform the duties for the position required.
Citizen/Nationality Must be a Kenyan citizen.
Education "O" Level, Proficiency in both Kiswahili and English.
Criminal History Clean Criminal record - Clean police clearance Certificate.
Fingerprints Candidate's submission of fingerprints of all fingers from both hands.
