About Us

Izomax Security Limited is duly incorporated and registered under the Companies Act, Chapter 486 Laws of Kenya. We provide a wide range of Security and Safety services such as guarding, supply, installation and maintenance of security and Fire Safety equipment. Our service packages are designed especially for the client with respect to their specific needs and financial capabilities. When we provide our services, we take the time to understand your needs and your environment as carry out full risk assessment to ensure our solution will fit your requirements. Our management team have the necessary experience and expertise to give you the best advice possible and the knowledge to consult and recommend suitable solutions for your unique needs.

Vision

To be a leading professional security and safety company in the provision of integrated, affordable and customer focused security services within Kenya and beyond.

Mission

To provide our customers with the best possible security and advisory services driven by passion to solve operational and security issues promptly, efficiently and at a reasonable cost.
